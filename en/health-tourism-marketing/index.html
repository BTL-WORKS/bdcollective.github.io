Health Tourism Marketing.

End-to-end marketing partnership for clinics serving international patients. Hair transplant, dental, aesthetic, IVF, bariatric. Istanbul × Dubai.

What this service is

An end-to-end marketing partnership for clinics that serve international patients. Hair transplant, dental, aesthetic surgery, IVF, bariatric, ophthalmology — built around the unique requirements of cross-border patient acquisition.

The work covers multilingual content, international SEO, paid media in target languages, multilingual WhatsApp Business operations, regulatory compliance across Turkey + EU + GDPR, and integration with the clinic's operational infrastructure (transfers, hotels, translators).

Who this is for

  • Clinics already serving international patients who want to reduce facilitator dependence and build direct demand.
  • Clinics ready to enter health tourism but unsure how to start.
  • Established clinics looking to expand into new target markets (UK, Germany, Gulf, etc.).

How it works — six stages

1. Clinic diagnosis and target-market selection — analysing the clinic, picking 2-3 priority markets.

2. Brand strategy and regulatory framework — multi-regulator compliance built in from day one.

3. Multilingual content and international SEO infrastructure — website architecture, hreflang, native-speaker editorial.

4. Paid media and organic flow — Google Ads, Meta Ads, YouTube Ads in target languages.

5. Multilingual WhatsApp Business and patient communication — API setup, language-specific reps, CRM integration.

6. Measurement, reporting, and continuity — qualified message flow, consultation rate, deposit conversion, LTV.

The complete picture
