Approach

Strategy. Story. Touch.

Boutique social media management for doctors and clinics covers four pillars: brand strategy, content production (Instagram Reels, video editing, photography, copywriting), Meta and Google Ads management, and continuous account stewardship with monthly reporting.

01.

Strategy

Understanding your brand, mapping your target patient, building a plan for the right message at the right moment.

↗

02.

Content

Shooting, editing, copywriting, visual design. Every frame passes through our hands. Custom, not stock.

↗

03.

Paid Media

Meta & Google Ads management. We sharpen the target audience and channel the budget where it matters most.

↗

04.

Continuity

Single point of management. Personal attention. Monthly reports. No surprises — just rhythm.

↗

Philosophy

An octopus doesn't swim with one arm.

Strategy, content, paid media, continuity — they cannot be separated. They are arms of one body. The moment you detach one from another, the work falls short.

Read our philosophy →

"Here, everything originates from one mind. While the shoot is happening, the creative for next month's campaign is already in motion. When a video is being edited, its caption is already being drafted."
