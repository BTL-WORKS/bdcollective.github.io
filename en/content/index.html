Method · 02

Content.

Shooting, editing, copywriting, visual design. Every frame passes through our hands. Custom, not stock. Healthcare-aware, surgeon-led.

Yayınlanma: 2026-06-01 Son güncelleme: 2026-06-26 Yazar: Yunus Badeci

How we think about content

A clinic's content is editorial, not industrial. Ten precisely crafted pieces outperform a hundred mediocre ones. We work in small batches with sharp intent — each piece serves a specific message from the strategy, speaks to a specific patient archetype, and passes through a regulatory filter before it goes live.

The surgeon is the central figure. Patients don't choose a clinic — they choose a doctor. Content that puts the clinician at the centre builds the trust that converts.

What we make

Reels and short video

The dominant format for new patient reach in 2026. We shoot on location at the clinic with the clinician present, edit in-house, write captions that hold up to regulatory review, and deliver in monthly batches.

Long-form video

For YouTube and long Reels. Treatment explainers, surgeon narratives, patient-journey documentaries (with documented consent). The kind of content that drives organic search and builds authority over time.

Posts and carousels

Educational, behind-the-scenes, surgeon perspective, clinic atmosphere. Designed to build depth with the existing audience.

Stories and daily content

Quick touchpoints. Surgeon at the clinic, patient feedback (consent-cleared), behind-the-scenes moments, Q&A. Always lightweight, never sloppy.

Photography

Clinical portraits, clinic atmosphere, surgical setup, equipment, team. Used across the website, paid media, and social media. Custom, not stock — patients can tell the difference.

Copy

Every caption, every video voiceover, every landing-page block. Written to match the clinic's voice and pass regulatory review. The unsexy work that decides whether content converts.

"Custom, not stock. Every frame passes through our hands."

How we work

One planned shoot day per month at the clinic. The surgeon's time is the scarcest resource — we plan around it tightly. From a single shoot day we produce a month of content: Reels, posts, stories, photos, snippets for paid media.

Edits and copy happen in our studio. The clinic reviews and approves a monthly batch ahead of publishing. Surprises are minimised.

What we don't do

  • AI-generated copy that sounds artificial — the patient feels it.
  • Stock photography of generic clinics — credibility evaporates.
  • "Guarantees" or comparative claims — regulation forbids them.
  • High-volume low-quality output — better fewer pieces, sharper.
